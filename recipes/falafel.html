Falafel

Description

A typical dish from the middle-east, falafel are deliciously crispy deep-fried fritters made with chickpeas that are rarely attempted at home but are actually very simple to make.

Ingredients

  • Dried chickpeas 500g
  • Garlic 1 clove
  • Cumin powder 1 pinch
  • Black pepper 1 pinch
  • 1 small onion
  • Parsley 1 bunch
  • fine salt 1 pinch

Steps

  1. Soak the dried chickpeas in cold water for at least 12 hours. Dry them, rinse them and then dry them again with a clean cloth.
  2. Pour the chickpeas into the mixer, add onion (chopped into coarse pieces) together with a crushed or squeezed garlic clove.
  3. Season with salt, pepper and cumin.
  4. Turn on the mixer to chop all the ingredients.
  5. Chop the parsley separately and add it to the mixture.
  6. Blend again to combine everything, then transfer the resulting mixture into a baking dish and compact it with the back of a spoon or a spatula.
  7. Cover with cling film and let it rest in the refrigerator for at least an hour.
